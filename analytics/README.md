## Risk & Analytics

Data science assets for portfolio analytics, reporting, and machine learning.

- `models/`: Statistical and ML models for valuation, VaR, liquidity, scenario analysis.
- `pipelines/`: Dataflow, Dataproc, and batch jobs feeding BigQuery/Firestore read models.
- `dashboards/`: Looker Studio/Grafana definitions for risk and treasury metrics.
- `notebooks/`: Exploratory research, Vertex AI notebooks, proof-of-concepts.
- `infra/`: Terraform, schemas, IAM policies supporting analytics workloads.
