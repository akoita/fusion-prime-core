## Cross-Chain Integrations

Bridge and relayer tooling to connect Fusion Prime contracts with external chains and providers.

- `relayers/`: Deterministic message relayers, retry workers, monitoring agents.
- `adapters/`: Protocol-specific clients (Axelar, CCIP, bespoke custodians).
- `monitoring/`: Health checks, dashboards, alerts for cross-chain finality.
- `runbooks/`: Operational playbooks for failover, incident response, and upgrades.
