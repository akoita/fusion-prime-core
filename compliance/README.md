## Compliance & Identity

Policy automation, case management, and reporting assets.

- `rules/`: Policy-as-code (e.g., CEL, Rego) for KYC/KYB, AML, sanctions.
- `workflows/`: Automation definitions for onboarding, investigations, SAR filing.
- `reporting/`: Scheduled reports, regulatory exports, evidence packages.
- `audit/`: Audit trail storage, attestations, runbooks for regulators.
